Delicious Cake for Everyone

Any celebration, whether a cozy gathering or an extravagent party, is incomplete without cake! We offer a wide range of handcrafted speciality cakes that are a treat to the eyes and also the taste buds.

Our Exclusive Products

truffle cake

Our signature offering, the Eggless Dutch Truffle Cake is made with a moist chocolate sponge and a rich chocolate truffle ganache, and finished with rice crisps for that extra texture. It's nostalgia on a plate, reminding us of the classic layered chocolate cake we all grew up eating!

Blueberry cake

Cream cheese delicately mixed with whipped dairy cream scented with lemon and vanilla, layered on a buttery biscuit base.Each bite is an explosion of different flavours, Topped with a blueberry glaze, this blueberry cheesecake is going to be your new favourity!

Chocoholic cake

A sight to behold, our Chocoholic Cake is the perfect treat for chocolate lovers. A sponge base is layered with dark chocolate mousse, then milk chocolate mousse, and finished with a layer of white chocolate mousse. Each bite is an explosion of three different chocolate flavours in the mouth.

Redvelvet cake

our Red Velvet cake is made by layering a cocoa and buttermilk sponge with our signature cream cheese & lime juice frosting. It is slightly sour in order to balance the sweetness of the cake. The hint creaminess and sweetness, leading to the perfectly balanced red velvet cake.

Hazelnut Cake

For our Hazelnut Praline Mousse Cake, a homemade hazelnut praline chocolate mousse is sandwiched between layers of moist chocolate sponge, and finished with a smooth hazelnut chocolate glaze along with toasted hazelnuts.

Pineapple cake

A fruity delight, our Fresh Cream Pineapple Cake is made by layering a vanilla sponge with fresh dairy cream and our homemade pineapple compote, and finished with slivered almonds. This tropical treat is moist and light, rounding off all the flavours perfectly

lemon tart

Our Lemon Tart is the balance of tangy and sweet flavours. A golden brown butter pastry is filled with a rich and creamy lemon curd and garnished with a bit of white chocolate. It makes for a wonderful dessert when craving something other than chocolate

Chocolate tart

The signature Chocolate Tart is a crisp butter pastry shell encasing a rich and undulgent chocolate ganache, and topped with rice crisps. This eggless decadent dessert is sure to make you swoon any time you are looking for a chocolate fix!

blueberry cheese Cupcakes

RIch & creamy cheesecake with blueberries on a cookie base, topped with a blueberry glaze, finished with blueberry frosting & cream. A perfect dessert for one!.
